Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 March 2025

The short version: TabFix stores your profile data locally on your own device using Chrome's built-in storage. We collect nothing. We send nothing to any server. There is no account, no tracking, and no analytics. Your data never leaves your browser.

1. Who we are

TabFix is a Chrome browser extension that auto-fills web forms using profile data you provide. It is developed and maintained as an independent project. For questions, contact wushumaster75@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

We collect no data whatsoever. TabFix has no servers, no database, no analytics pipeline, and no third-party SDKs.

The profile information you enter into TabFix (name, email, address, phone number, etc.) is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your own device. This storage is:

  • Accessible only by the TabFix extension on your device
  • Never transmitted over the network
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Deleted immediately when you uninstall the extension or clear the storage manually

3. Permissions we request and why

  • storage — to save your profiles and fill-count statistics locally on your device
  • activeTab — to read and fill the form fields on the page you are currently viewing, only when you trigger a fill action
  • scripting — to inject the form-filling script when triggered by the hotkey (Alt+Shift+F) or the popup button
  • tabs — to identify the active tab so the hotkey can target the correct page

None of these permissions are used to collect, transmit, or monitor your browsing activity.

4. Third-party services

TabFix Pro upgrades are processed by Stripe. When you click "Upgrade to Pro" you are redirected to Stripe's hosted payment page. TabFix does not receive or store your payment card details. Please review Stripe's privacy policy for how they handle payment data.

The TabFix landing page (this website) is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser type) as described in GitHub's privacy statement.

5. Children's privacy

TabFix is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Email wushumaster75@gmail.com.
